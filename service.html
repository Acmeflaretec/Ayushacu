Our Treatments


Ayurveda

We offer personalized Ayurvedic treatments for holistic health and well-being, focusing on balancing the body's natural energies through diet, herbal medicine, and traditional therapies.



Unani (Hijama)

Traditionally, hijama treats various health conditions, including musculoskeletal pain, respiratory problems, digestive disorders, and skin problems. Modern medicine recognises Hijama therapy as reducing pain and inflammation in the body, improving blood circulation, and boosting the immune system.



Kalari Marma

Kalari Marma focuses on manipulating vital energy points (Marmas) to alleviate pain and restore physical and emotional well-being.



Ortho Sports Injuries

We offer specialized treatments for sports-related injuries, combining physiotherapy, Ayurveda, and advanced rehabilitation techniques to enhance recovery and performance.



Spine and Joint Care

Our spine and joint care program focuses on treating chronic pain, arthritis, and joint problems using a holistic approach, integrating Ayurveda and modern therapies.



Bone Alignment

Our bone alignment services help restore skeletal balance and posture, promoting optimal body function and relieving pain.



Chiropractic

Our chiropractic services focus on spinal adjustments to alleviate pain, improve posture, and enhance overall health.



Stroke Recovery

Our stroke recovery program combines Ayurvedic therapies, physical therapy, and rehabilitative exercises to restore functional abilities and enhance recovery after a stroke.



Piles Treatment

We provide natural treatment for piles (hemorrhoids) using Ayurvedic therapies to reduce discomfort and promote healing.



Varicose Veins Treatment

Our varicose veins treatment includes Ayurvedic remedies and lifestyle modifications to alleviate pain and improve circulation.



Skin Treatments

We offer a range of Ayurvedic skin treatments to address skin issues like acne, pigmentation, and aging, using natural and herbal remedies for a radiant and healthy complexion.
